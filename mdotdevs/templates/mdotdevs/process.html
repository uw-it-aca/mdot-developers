UW Developer Store Publishing Process

So, you have developed a native app and want to publish it under the UW Developer Store? Here is what you do next.

  1. Step 1

    Confirm that your app meets the UW Developer Store Publishing Guidelines.

  2. Step 2

    Submit your application for UW-IT review.
    • UW-IT will determine if publishing guidelines are met.
    • A UW-IT Usability review will be scheduled.

  3. Step 3

    Fix critical issues brought up in the UW-IT Usability Review.

  4. Step 4

    Alert UW-IT Mobile Team that your app is ready for final review.
    • UW-IT conducts final app review.
    • App submitted by UW-IT to the appropriate app stores.
    • Publication, pending app store approval.
